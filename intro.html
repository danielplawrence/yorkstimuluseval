Hi! Thanks for agreeing to evaluate my stimuli! Please read the following text carefully:

In the following test you will hear a number of isolated words, including a mix of synthetic, semisynthetic and natural items. These words will be used in a perception test which explores listeners' ability to extract social information from speech.

Each time you hear a word, please evaluate it on the scales provided. I'm particularly interested in how natural you think the speech sounds -- if some of my items are more natural than others, this could bias my results.

After the experiment has finished, you will be asked some basic questions about your language background.
