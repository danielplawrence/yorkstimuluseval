Thank you for taking part in this study

The images you evaluated in this test will be used as part of a speech perception experiment where listeners will hear voices and try to match them to different people.

In this way, we are able to test how people associate different ways of speaking with different social characteristics.

Collecting ratings of the faces beforehand will allow us to account for reactions people might have to the faces -- for example, to control for the effect of facial attractiveness.
